Tagore Charith Chepuri snips a play

MANIKIN AND MINIKIN by _Alfred Kreymborg_
SHE.Manikin!
>
HE. Minikin?
SHE.

That fool of a servant has done it again.

HE.

I should say, she's more than a fool.

SHE.

A meddlesome busybody----

HE.

A brittle-fingered noddy!

SHE.

Which way are you looking? What do you see?

SHE. Manikin!

[_The clock goes on ticking for a moment. Its mellow chimes strike the hour._]
